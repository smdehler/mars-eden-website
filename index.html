Mission Overview

The Mars Eden Initiative (MEI) is an international coalition of scientists and engineers tasked with developing the first sustainable agriculture systems on Mars. Our mission is to support long-term human habitation through innovation in extraterrestrial horticulture and closed-loop agriculture.

Who We’re Looking For

We are currently seeking highly qualified individuals with graduate-level expertise in:

  • Botany, Agronomy, or Environmental Biology
  • Aerospace Engineering or Systems Design
  • Astrobiology or Space Agriculture
Applicants with previous experience in space missions or Mars analog environments are strongly encouraged to apply.

What You’ll Receive

Successful candidates will:

  • Undergo two years of advanced astronaut and agricultural systems training on Earth
  • Join the first permanent Mars colony with full residential status
  • Participate in groundbreaking scientific research with global recognition
  • Receive full mission support and long-term innovation funding

How to Apply

Submit your CV, academic credentials, and a personal mission statement via our secure online portal. Shortlisted applicants will be invited to interview at our Earth-based Space Training Complex.
